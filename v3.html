Our Philosophy

We believe that the world is more peaceful when global leaders strive to understand one another, despite possibly divergent values. We have created the Asia Leaders Series to act as a forum for open dialogue between Europe and Asia, in an effort to provide policy-makers, economists and business leaders with a safe place to address misconceptions, form new relationships, and align on pressing world issues.
